Become Aria Member

  1. Home
  2. Members
  3. Become Aria Member

Imagine if you could connect with fellow RIAs, build a robust practice, gain knowledge, access opportunities and achieve success.

It’s possible by becoming an ARIA member!

Being a member not only helps you be a part of a community of RIAs, it helps you to grow and strengthen your practice. For its members, ARIA organizes the following activities:

  • Annual Flagship Conference which we call ‘RIA Summit
    • During Covid-19, we have organized mini-Summits
  • Mini Conferences
  • Weekly engagement sessions targeted at professional development of RIAs covering a broad range of subjects (knowledge building, management concepts, technology related, practitioners’ insights, compliance, etc)
  • In addition, local chapters also organize member activities

Being a member entitles you to access ARIA’s resources on compliance with SEBI regulations which includes webinars with industry experts, compliance calendar, draft letter of engagement, etc. You can also access learning resources on ARIA’s website (archives of our knowledge sessions).

Being a member gives you an opportunity to network with fellow ARIAs. You can ask/discuss technical and practice related questions/topics to other ARIA members on our Whatsapp group.

If you’re still not convinced, read what happy members of ARIA have to say.

Become a member and join the ARIA movement today!
Apply Now

The Association of Registered Investment Advisers (ARIA) was born out of the need to support the development of the investment adviser community post the introduction of the investment advisor regulations of SEBI in 2013.
